Background

Thermal Engineering Design for Additive Manufacturing

Qdot Additive LLC uses advanced simulation tools and years of know-how in additive manufacturing to design innovative structures for heat transfer applications, from HVAC&R to high temperature recuperators and power generation applications.

Projects

Polymer Composite Air-to-Water Heat Exchanger

Qdot Additive designed a low pressure drop air-to-water heat exchanger for HVAC applications. It is manufactured by fused filament fabrication using a thermally conductive polymer composite also developed in-house.

Stainless Steel Air-to-Water Heat Exchanger

Qdot Additive designed a low pressure drop air-to-water heat exchanger for HVAC applications. It is manufactured by the direct metal laser sintering (DMLS) process using stainless steel 316L.
